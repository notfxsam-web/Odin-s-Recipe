Samosa

Here are the exact ingredients to make a simple samosa

  • Maida(2 Cup)
  • Oil/ghee
  • salt(3/4tsp)
  • Water(4-6 tbsp)
  • Filling Ingredients(Potato,Green Peas,Spices etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Prepare the Filling
  3. Shape the Samosa
  4. Roll: Divide the dough into equal balls. Roll one ball into an oval shape (about 6-7 inches).
  5. Cut: Cut the oval in half to create two semi-circles.
  6. Cone: Apply water to the straight edge of a semi-circle. Bring the edges together to form a cone, sealing the pointy end firmly.
  7. Fill: Stuff with 1-2 tbsp of filling.
  8. Seal: Apply water on the inner edges. Make a small pleat on the edge that doesn't have the seam, then seal the back side to the front, creating a triangular shape.
  9. Fry The Samosa

Now your Samosa is ready to Eat
