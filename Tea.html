Tea

So here are the exact ingredients you need to make a Tea

  • Milk
  • Tea powder
  • Water
  • Sweetener(Sugar,Brown Sugar etc.)

Steps

  1. Boil Water: Heat water, adding fresh ginger and spices early to allow them to steep.
  2. Add Tea & Sugar: Add tea powder/leaves and sugar, boiling for a few minutes
  3. Add Milk: Pour in milk and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Strain & Serve: Strain the tea into cups to remove the leaves and spices.

Now Enjoy Your Tea
